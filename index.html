Nice to meet you

My mission is to build product that users want and need

I'm a Product Designer based in Abeokuta, Nigeria. I specialise in UX/UI design, brand strategy, and product management. I cofounded Homlify with two of my great friends and I’m currently doing my MBA at the University of Nexford, Washington DC. I'm always striving to grow and learn something new and I don't take myself too seriously. I'm passionate about helping startups grow, improve their customer experience, and to raise venture capital through good design.
